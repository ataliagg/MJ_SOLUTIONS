How We Work

A team built around reliability, detail, and trust.

Since 2012, MJ Cleaning Solutions has grown by building repeatable systems and showing up consistently for offices, schools, healthcare spaces, and construction teams.

We do not treat cleaning like a one-size-fits-all service. Each job starts with a quick walk-through, a clear scope, and a plan for access, timing, and priorities. From there, the crew works through the site in a logical order, checks the details before finishing, and keeps communication open so the client always knows what was done and what comes next.

That process matters because different spaces need different rhythms. A school may need fast turnaround and visibility, a healthcare space may need tighter sanitation steps, and a post-construction site may need heavy detail cleaning before handoff. Our team is built to adjust to that without losing consistency.
